Wayne (Wen-Yen) Wu

I am a Research Scientist at University of California, Los Angeles, working with Bolei Zhou. Since 2017, I have been a cooperative researcher at MMLab, working closely with Dahua Lin and Xiaogang Wang. In June 2022, I obtained my PhD in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. From 2019 to 2020, I was pleasure to work with Chen Change Loy at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My research lies at the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics, especially in the digital human generation and simulation.
